How do we work?

The car buying process usually begins with identifying your needs and preferences. This includes determining your budget, vehicle type (e.g. sedan, SUV, hatchback, etc.), make, model, desired features and options, and other criteria that are important to you.

You can find out more about the work process of our company below in the steps by which the entire process takes place:

  1. Vehicle Purchasing: The company purchases vehicles from manufacturers, dealers or through auctions. In some cases, companies also accept trade-in cars from customers when purchasing a new car.
  2. Vehicle service and preparation: Purchased vehicles may require service or preparation for sale. This may include cleaning, repairs, replacement of parts and other necessary measures to improve the condition of the vehicle before sale.
  3. Car sales: The company develops marketing and advertising strategies to attract potential buyers. The company may also offer financial services, such as financing or leasing, to help customers purchase a car.
  4. Customer Service: Once a vehicle is sold, the company can provide warranty, technical support, parts and repair services.
  5. Accounting and Administrative Work: The company also handles accounting and administrative work such as paperwork, vehicle registration, financial accounting, etc.

Buying a car is a confident step in life because it can be difficult and expensive, so it is important to carefully consider your circumstances before making a decision.
